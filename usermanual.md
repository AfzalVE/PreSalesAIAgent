# User Manual: AI Proposal Broker System

Welcome to the AI Proposal Broker system. This manual provides a step-by-step guide on how to navigate the application, create new proposals, and access administrative features.

---

## 1. Application Entry
1. Open the application in your web browser.
2. On the landing page, click the **Get Started** button to proceed to the authentication screen.

---

## 2. Client Authentication

You can access the client portal either by creating a new account or using the pre-configured demo account.

### Option A: New User Signup
1. Toggle to the **Sign Up** view.
2. Provide a **genuine email address**.
3. Check your inbox and **verify the OTP** to activate your account.

## 3. Generating a Proposal

Once authenticated, you will land on the **Pre Sales Dashboard** (Client Dashboard). 

1. Click on the **New Proposal Request** button.
2. Choose your preferred intake method:
   - **Form Fill:** Manually fill out the project details form with your requirements.
   - **AI Broker (Recommended):** Click **Skip to Broker Chat** to bypass the form and interact directly with the AI.
3. If you chose the Broker Chat, converse with the AI to negotiate your project's scope, timeline, and budget.
4. When you are satisfied with the estimates, instruct the AI to proceed (e.g., type *"ok generate the poc"*).
5. The system will generate your final proposal (POC). You can view the full details and click **Create Final POC** / **Download POC** from the right-hand panel.
